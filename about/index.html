The name of this site comes from a song called Norwegian Wood, composed by The Beatles from their 1965 album Rubber Soul. I am captivated by the special feeling of the song, and I love its subtle sense of sadness. Additionally, I am a big fan of other Beatles classics such as Julia, And I Love Her, and While My Guitar Gently Weeps.

Norwegian Wood (ノルウェイの森) is also a 1987 novel by Haruki Murakami. I read this novel in 2020 and I also began writing blogs that year. 小林緑 is my favorite character in this novel. I wrote a blog about the songs composed by The Beatles in this novel here.

I am a student and I love music and coding. Recently, I’ve been playing guitar and jogging.

Have a great time here and feel free to share your site below.
